Calgary Auto Repair Available

When it comes to tires and auto repair, no one can beat Tire Pirates. We offer top of the line tires at great prices, but we are more than just tires. Our repair services are done by highly-trained technicians who do quality work. These services are also offered at a great price.

Our Calgary auto repair and tire services include:

  • Tire repair: A barely used tire does not have to be replaced when damaged. Minor damage to a tire can be repaired.
  • Out of province inspections: Have your inspection done by a highly licensed automotive technician.
  • Wheel alignments: Drifting issues can be fixed with an alignment. Our technicians are quick and efficient with repairs.
  • Tune-ups: We will inspect everything under the hood to ensure that no belts, spark plugs, or wires are broken or nearly broken.
  • New and used tire sales: We strive to meet all budgets, which is why we offer both new and used quality tires.
  • Wheel balancing: Avoid vibrations in your steering. Get your wheels balanced by our trained mechanics.
  • Steering and suspension service: It should never be a struggle to get your car moving. Our technicians can keep your steering and suspension in shape.
  • Brake service: Bad brakes can lead to bad accidents. Get those brakes serviced before itâ€™s too late.
  • Struts and shock absorber repair and replacement: Keep your ride smooth with the help of our well-trained technicians.
  • Cooling system maintenance: Prevent overheating and increase the life of your engine by maintaining the cooling system.
  • Heating and air conditioning: You should never have to freeze in winter and fry in the summer. Keep your heat and air conditioning systems running.

It is important to us to offer the best in customer care, which is why we strive to offer the top brands of tires. At our locations in Chinook or Foothills, you can find BF Goodrich, Bridgestone, Cooper, Firestone, Goodyear, Hankook, Michelin, Pirelli, Toyo, UniRoyal, Westlake, and Yokohama.
